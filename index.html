About

We want to showcase the work of some of the best photographers from India. This is a unique exhibition where each photographer showcases their best image. The first of these exhibitions will be held in April 2018 in the Habitat Centre in New Delhi.

The theme for the photographs is "Nature and Travel". We have invited photographers from all over India to send us up to three of their best images. Out of the three we will select one image to showcase in the exhibition.

With the permission of the photographers, we intend to sell their images to raise funds for conservation awareness campaigns. Details of the campaigns will be shared when the exhibit opens.

Guidelines

Photographers must upload their photographs as digital files with specifications as follows -

  • RGB (sRGB or Adobe RGB)
  • 36 inches on the longest side
  • 300 PPI
  • Saved as a TIFF or JPEG at a setting 10 or higher
  • Please do not watermark your images
